## 1. Project Overview
The objective of Lab 3 was to design and build a fully responsive **Product Landing Page** strictly using **Bootstrap 5**. The chosen product is a next-generation "AI Smart Lock" (AEGIS.AI). The design focuses on a modern, high-tech aesthetic with ample whitespace, dark backgrounds, and vibrant tech-blue accents.

## 2. Academic Requirements Met
To meet the strict grading criteria, this landing page successfully integrates the **8 required Bootstrap components**:

1.  **Navbar:** A `fixed-top` responsive navigation bar with a collapsible hamburger menu for mobile devices.
2.  **Carousel:** A dynamic hero section featuring a fading image slider (`carousel-fade`) that introduces the product's core values.
3.  **Grid System:** Extensive use of Bootstrap's responsive grid (`row`, `col-12`, `col-md-6`, `col-lg-4`) to ensure perfect display across mobile, tablet, and desktop viewports.
4.  **Cards:** Utilized in the "Core Architecture" section to neatly display technical specifications.
5.  **Badges:** Integrated within the feature cards (e.g., "Proprietary", "Mil-Spec") to highlight key tech specs.
6.  **Buttons:** Custom-styled Bootstrap buttons (`btn-custom-primary`, `btn-custom-secondary`) used as Call-to-Actions (CTAs) throughout the page.
7.  **Accordion:** Implemented in the "System Inquiries" (FAQ) section to manage vertical space efficiently.
8.  **Form:** A complete "Waitlist Protocol" form utilizing Bootstrap's form controls for clean, aligned inputs.

*Additionally, a distinct **Footer** section was included at the bottom to complete the structural requirement.*

## 3. UI/UX Highlights
* **Immersive Dark Theme:** Built on a custom color palette utilizing deep grays (`#0b0f19`) and neon cyan (`#00e5ff`) to simulate a cybersecurity/AI environment.
* **Micro-Interactions:** Custom hover states on cards (lifting effect) and buttons (glow effect) to provide tactile feedback without relying on heavy JavaScript.
* **Accessibility:** Maintained high contrast ratios between text and background elements.
